Synopsis

When Azrael Myers arrives at his college dorm to begin his freshman year studying Osteopathic Medicine, he's surprised to find he has a roommate—Biene Demetros, a quirky Naturopathic Medicine student with a penchant for beekeeping-inspired fashion. Their initial awkward meeting evolves into a strong partnership when they discover a mysterious infection spreading across campus, turning students into plant-like zombies. After their friend Jasper falls victim to the strange disease, Biene and Azrael uncover that Nurse Nightingale is behind the outbreak. Armed with their unique abilities—Biene's Botanokinesis allowing her to control plants and Azrael's Mortokinesis giving him power over death—they confront the nurse and discover a sinister plot that threatens the entire student body. As skeptical campus security slowly realizes the severity of the situation, Biene and Azrael take matters into their own hands, working tirelessly in a makeshift lab to develop a cure. Their solution—an aerosol treatment dispersed through the campus ventilation system—successfully counters the infection, saving countless lives. Four years later, at their graduation ceremony, the pair reflects on their extraordinary journey from reluctant roommates to campus heroes. Though the plant zombie incident has faded into campus legend, the experience has forged an unbreakable bond between them, preparing them for whatever challenges await in their medical careers.

Character List

  • Biene Demetros – A Naturopathic Medicine student with Botanokinesis (plant manipulation abilities). Known for her unique fashion sense and caring nature. She's level-headed in a crisis and deeply connected to nature.
  • Azrael Myers – An Osteopathic Medicine student focusing on Preventive Medicine. Possesses Mortokinesis (death manipulation powers). Initially skeptical and slightly standoffish, he develops into a reliable partner who doesn't hesitate to take action when needed.
  • Jasper Stone – A friend of Biene who becomes one of the first victims of the plant infection. Their sickness serves as the catalyst for Biene and Azrael's investigation.
  • Nurse Nightingale – The antagonist who spreads the plant infection throughout campus. Her true motives remain mysterious, as she refuses to explain her actions when confronted.
  • Professor Aello – A half-harpy professor who is turned into one of the plant zombies and later restrained by Biene.
  • Unnamed Professor – An older faculty member who helps Biene and Azrael in their quest to develop a cure, offering them lab resources despite his skepticism.

Key Moments

  • First Meeting - Azrael discovers Biene is his roommate, setting up their unexpected partnership.
  • Patient Zero - Jasper falls ill during class, marking the beginning of the outbreak.
  • Discovery - Biene notices multiple students becoming sick after visiting Nurse Nightingale.
  • Confrontation - The duo confronts Nurse Nightingale and discovers her role in the infection.
  • The Chase - Azrael pursues and temporarily neutralizes Nightingale while Biene restrains the infected Professor Aello.
  • Lab Work - Weeks of intensive research as they develop a cure for the infected students.
  • The Cure - Their aerosol treatment successfully reverses the infection across campus.
  • Graduation - Four years later, they reflect on their journey and look toward the future, their bond stronger than ever.
