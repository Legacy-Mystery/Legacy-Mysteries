Synopsis

Biene, Azrael, and Amelia never expected their routine lives to be shattered by an ancient conspiracy. When a series of unnatural attacks lead them to a long-forgotten ruin, they uncover more than just history—secrets of a buried war, the name of a fallen god, and a power that refuses to stay sealed. As they delve deeper into the mystery, the trio braves deadly traps and relentless hunters, finally retrieving Draconyx’s lost staff—an artifact tied to a failed rebellion and a goddess who may still be pulling the strings. Forced to flee their home, they seek refuge in Crimson Hollow, only to find themselves branded criminals for carrying an unregistered dark magic relic. Their salvation comes in the form of an unlikely ally—someone who knows more than they should and may have their own stake in this unfolding battle. With the forces of Ethyros and Typhora closing in, the trio—and their newest companion—must decide: will they continue running, or will they fight to uncover the truth before history repeats itself?

Chapter Overviews

  • First Case - The trio investigates a mysterious attack, uncovering the first clues about the hidden dangers in their world.
  • Farm Affairs - Their search for answers leads them to Biene’s farm, where strange plant-based infections raise new questions.
  • Market Murmurs - While gathering supplies and information at the market, they encounter unexpected allies and enemies.
  • Shadows Below - They explore underground ruins, facing supernatural threats and uncovering a secretive faction.
  • Embered Fang - Solving puzzles within the ruins, they discover Draconyx’s staff and unwittingly alert the hunters to their presence.
  • Crimson Flight - Fleeing to Crimson Hollow, they are pursued and forced to navigate a dangerous new city.
  • Hollow Intrigue - They meet the fourth protagonist, who pulls them into the hidden world of underground resistance.
  • Secrets in the Dark - Learning more about Draconyx’s past, they start forming a plan to counter Ethyros’ cultists.
  • Echoes of Rebellion - The group infiltrates a cultist hideout, retrieving valuable information but at a cost.
  • Rising Tensions - Internal conflicts threaten to split the group, but they must stay united as threats close in.
  • Trial of Fire - To earn the trust of an old ally, they undergo a series of challenges testing their abilities and resolve.
  • Whispers of War - They uncover the extent of Ethyros’ forces and realize the gods themselves may be involved.
  • Broken Chains - A daring mission puts them in direct conflict with Typhora’s forces, changing their understanding of the battle ahead.
  • Storm’s Edge - The mentor figure enters, offering guidance but also revealing difficult truths about the gods’ war.
  • Gathering Shadows - The quintet prepares for their final confrontation, seeking out remaining allies and tools.
  • The Reckoning - Ethyros and Typhora launch their final attempt to free Draconyx, forcing the group into a desperate battle.
  • The Shattered Seal - Despite their best efforts, the villains succeed, and Draconyx is freed, leaving the group with an impossible choice.
  • Fate’s Divide - The world begins to shift as the power balance changes, and the quintet must decide their next steps.
  • Legacy’s Dawn - The first book closes with the group setting out on a new mission, knowing the battle has only begun.

Character List

  • Azrael Myers – A mortokinetic who manipulates death energy and commands eerie shadows and spirits.
  • Biene Demetros – A calm and grounded botanokinetic who can control and communicate with plants.
  • Amelia Sabré – A bold and witty demigod who channels lightning, using her sword to harness electrical energy.
  • Einar Bjørnsson – An adventurous shapeshifter and cosmic mage with control over space and astral energy.
  • Alexios – A mysterious and analytical figure with psychic insight and a sharp instinct for hidden truths.
  • Shadow – Azrael’s loyal raven familiar, with ghostly feathers and the ability to phase through objects.
  • Thorn – Biene’s bee familiar, a glowing companion who pollinates magical flowers and warns of danger.
  • Valor – Amelia’s Saint Bernard familiar, strong and protective, who generates a faint aura of static electricity.
  • Whisper – Einar’s wise and quiet owl familiar, with star-speckled wings and a knack for finding hidden paths.
  • Ripple – Alexios’ playful otter familiar, who glides through water and spirit barriers with ease.
  • Draconyx – The god of dragons and forgotten rebellion, once sealed away, now stirring as his relic resurfaces.
  • Typhora – The goddess of rebellion and resistance, fiercely independent and bound to Draconyx’s fate.
  • Ethyros – The ruthless goddess of blood and sacrifice, manipulating cultists and hunters to revive lost power.
  • The Hunters – A secretive faction trained to neutralize threats tied to ancient relics and outlawed divine magic.
